## For Students

### Your Dashboard - What You'll See

When you log in as a student, you'll see your **Student Dashboard**. Here's what each section means:

#### Top Bar
- **Your name and avatar** (top left)
- **Star Points** ‚≠ê - Your reward points (like game currency!)
- **Level** - Your current level (higher = more progress!)
- **Streak** üî• - How many days in a row you've been active

#### Main Sections

---

### 1. Stellar Journey (Progress Chart) üìä

**What is it?** A graph showing your weekly progress over time.

**How to use it:**
- Look at the coloured bars - each colour is a different subject
- Taller bars = more progress that week
- Hover over bars to see exact numbers

---

### 2. Constellation Skill Tree ‚≠ê

**What is it?** A space map showing all the topics you're learning, displayed as star constellations!

**How to read it:**
- Each **star** = one topic
- **Brighter stars** = topics you've mastered
- **Dim stars** = topics you're still learning
- **Glowing stars** (90%+) = You've nearly perfected it!

**How to use it:**
1. Click **"View Full Skill Tree"**
2. Click on a subject tab (Maths, Physics, Economics)
3. See all your topics as stars connected by lines
4. Click any star to see the topic name and your mastery %

---

### 3. Command Center (Space Shop) üõ∏

**What is it?** A shop where you spend your Star Points on cool rewards!

**How to access it:**
1. Click the **"Command Center"** button on your dashboard

**What can you buy?**
- **Titles** - Custom titles that appear next to your name (e.g., "Star Captain")
- **Frames** - Fancy borders around your avatar
- **Avatars** - Different profile pictures
- **Spaceships** - Cool spaceship icons for your profile
- **Celebrations** - Special animations when you complete tasks
- **Boosters** - Temporary power-ups (e.g., 2x XP for a week!)

**How to buy:**
1. Browse items in the shop
2. Click on something you like
3. Check you have enough Star Points
4. Click **"Purchase"**
5. Done! Go to "My Inventory" to see your stuff

**How to equip items:**
1. Go to **"My Inventory"** tab
2. Find the item you want to use
3. Click **"Equip"**
4. The item is now active!

---

### 4. Leaderboard üèÜ

**What is it?** See how you rank against other students!

**Privacy:** Your real name is hidden - only your **Username** shows.

**How to set your username:**
1. Click on your profile
2. Set a fun username (max 20 characters)
3. This is what others see on the leaderboard

---

### 5. Assignments & Homework üìö

**What is it?** View and submit your homework.

**How to check homework:**
1. Look at the "Upcoming Assignments" section
2. See due dates and subjects
3. Click on an assignment to view details

**Tip:** Complete homework on time to build your **Homework Streak** and earn bonus points!

---

### 6. Notifications üîî

- Check the bell icon for updates
- See reminders about upcoming sessions
- Get alerts about new assignments

## For Tutors

### Your Dashboard - Overview

When you log in as a tutor, you'll see your **Tutor Dashboard** with these sections:

---

### 1. Your Students List üë•

**What is it?** A list of all students assigned to you.

**What you can see:**
- Student name
- Year group
- Subjects they're enrolled in
- Overall progress percentage
- Star points and level

**How to use it:**
1. Click on any student row to see their full profile
2. Use the search bar to find specific students
3. Filter by subject or year group

---

### 2. Topic Mastery Management ‚≠ê

**What is it?** This is where you update how well each student has mastered each topic. This directly updates their Constellation Skill Tree!

**How to access it:**
1. Click the **"Topic Mastery"** button (has a sparkle icon ‚ú®)
2. A popup window will open

**How to update a student's mastery:**

1. **Select a student** from the dropdown list
2. **Choose a subject** tab (Maths, Physics, Economics)
3. You'll see a list of all topics for that subject
4. For each topic, use the **slider** to set mastery:
   - **0%** = Not started
   - **25%** = Just beginning
   - **50%** = Half way there
   - **75%** = Good understanding
   - **100%** = Fully mastered! ‚≠ê
5. Add **optional notes** if needed
6. Click **"Save Changes"**

**What happens:** The student's skill tree will update immediately - mastered topics will glow!

---

### 3. Schedule Sessions üìÖ

**How to schedule a tutoring session:**
1. Click **"Schedule Session"**
2. Select the student
3. Pick a date and time
4. Choose the subject(s)
5. Add any notes
6. Click **"Confirm"**

---

### 4. Assign Homework üìù

**How to give homework:**
1. Click **"Assign Homework"**
2. Select the student(s)
3. Choose the subject and topic
4. Set a due date
5. Write instructions or attach files
6. Click **"Assign"**

---

### 5. Award Bonus Points üéÅ

**When to use:** Reward students for exceptional work!

**How to award points:**
1. Go to a student's profile
2. Click **"Award Bonus"**
3. Enter the number of Star Points
4. Select a reason (e.g., "Outstanding homework", "Great participation")
5. Click **"Award"**

---

### 6. Apply Penalties ‚ö†Ô∏è

**When to use:** For missed sessions or very low engagement.

**How it works:**
- The system can auto-detect low engagement
- You can manually apply penalties if needed
- Penalties reduce Star Points

## Gamification Features Explained

### Star Points ‚≠ê
- **Earn them by:** Completing homework, attending sessions, high scores
- **Spend them in:** Command Center shop
- **Lose them by:** Missing sessions (penalty)

### Levels
- Level up by earning XP
- Higher levels unlock more shop items
- Show off your dedication!

### Streaks üî•
- Build streaks by being active every day
- Longer streaks = bonus points
- Missing a day resets your streak!

### Weekly Progress
- Tracked automatically
- Displayed in the Stellar Journey chart
- Try to beat last week's progress!
